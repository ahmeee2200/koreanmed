Understanding Mental Health Disorders

What is Stress?

Stress is the body's response to any kind of demand or threat. It can be caused by external factors such as work, relationships, or health issues. While a certain amount of stress is normal, chronic stress can have negative effects on physical and mental health. üòü

What is Depression?

Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest. It affects how you feel, think, and handle daily activities. It can lead to physical problems and interfere with daily life. üòî

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease about something with an uncertain outcome. While it's normal to feel anxious in certain situations, excessive or chronic anxiety can interfere with your life. üò®
